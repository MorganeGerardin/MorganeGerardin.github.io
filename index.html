ABOUT


I am a post-doc in the STORM team, IRIT, Toulouse. My research is at the interface between Computer Graphics and Optics, focusing on the modeling of material appearance based on radiometric acquisitions. I am currently working with Mathias Paulin on the inverse rendering of translucent material appearance. I am also collaborating with Romain Pacanowski from Manao team, Inria, where I conducted a previous post-doc on the design of a calibrated opto-numerical system for the acquisition of translucent material appearance.
